Feed man's best friend

PhoneBone is a community initiative to provide street dogs with a consistent, nutritious diet, helping them stay happy and healthy as they wait to find a forever home. With help from our sponsors we dispense dog food out of booths set up on streets across cities in India. What's in it for the humankind? The booth acts as a mobile phone charging station, so while you feed your phone you are also contributing towards feeding a dog in need.

How it works

STEP 1
Running low on mobile battery?
Charge it at our Phone-Bone booth.

While you FEED your phone,
let's help FEED the
street dogs around us.

STEP 2
For every $1 paid to charge, the user
can dispense 1 kg of food and help
feed a street dog.

STEP 3
Press the button and dispense food.
You've saved a street dog from being malnourished.
